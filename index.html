Get Started

Add Web3 rewards to your app with a few lines of code

With our plug and play SDK, you can add crypto rewards to your app without hiring any blockchain devs.
Get Started
Embed Web3 Rewards
With X-to-Earn you can seamlessly create and add Web3 rewards to your app. Users can then easily earn rewards for various actions they perform on your app.
Easy to Manage
Manage your rewards from our dashboard without writing smart contracts or web3 code. Rewards are paid to users directly from your app for the various actions they carry out via our SDK.
X-to-Earn SDK
Our JavaScript SDK allows you to easily add a crypto rewards program to any app in a matter of minutes. We currently support NEAR and are looking to add other chains soon.

How does it work?

With X-to-Earn, you can add crypto rewards to your app in 3 easy steps. No Smart Contract development, no Web3 code. Easy to create, deploy and integrate.
Create a Rewards Program
Create a Rewards Program on our dashboard with Rewards users can claim along with the Amount per Reward & the Limit for the reward. e.g. reward_event_1| 0.5 NEAR | 200 would mean 200 people can claim 0.5 NEAR for reward_event_1
Deploy your Rewards Program
Once you have defined the Rewards Program along with all the Rewards, deposit the required NEAR & deploy your Rewards Program on chain from our dashboard
Users Claim Rewards
Integrate our SDK (x-to-earn-near-sdk) and add the function call for each reward event which pays the reward to the user. The function can be called via our SDK for almost any activity on your app.
